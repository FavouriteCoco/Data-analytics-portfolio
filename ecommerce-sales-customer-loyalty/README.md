E-Commerce Sales & Customer Loyalty Analysis

Project Overview
This project analyzes an e-commerce dataset to understand sales performance, customer behavior, and loyalty trends. The analysis focuses strictly on completed order transactions to ensure accurate revenue and customer metrics.

Objective
To identify key revenue drivers, analyze customer loyalty behavior, and evaluate channel and discount performance.

Tools Used
Excel, Power BI, Tableau

Key Metrics Analyzed
 • Total Revenue
 • Total Orders
 • Total Customers
 • Loyal Customer Revenue Percentage
 • Monthly Sales Trend
 • Sales by Channel

Key Insights
 • Loyal customers generated a significant share of total revenue.
 • Email and direct channels showed stronger repeat-purchase behavior.
 • Revenue trends indicated seasonal patterns.
 • Some discounts increased order volume but reduced repeat purchase rates.

Deliverables
 • Power BI dashboard
 • Tableau dashboard
 • Insight summary report
