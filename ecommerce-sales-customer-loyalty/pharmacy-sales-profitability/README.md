Pharmacy Sales & Profitability Analysis

Project Overview
This project analyzes sales and profitability data from a multi-country pharmacy network to evaluate business performance across regions, pharmacy types, and products.

Objective
To assess revenue and margin performance and understand the impact of promotions on sales and profitability.

Tools Used
Excel, Power BI

Key Metrics Analyzed
 • Total Revenue
 • Total Units Sold
 • Total Gross Margin
 • Gross Margin Percentage
 • Revenue by Country and Region
 • Promotion Impact on Sales

Key Insights
 • Urban pharmacies generated higher sales volume, while rural pharmacies showed stronger margin efficiency.
 • Revenue was concentrated among a few high-performing product categories.
 • Promotions increased volume but reduced margins in certain regions.
 • Some countries showed high revenue with lower profitability.

Deliverables
 • Power BI executive dashboard
 • Business insight report
